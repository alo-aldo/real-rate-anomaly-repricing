## Raw Data

Raw third-party data are not redistributed in this repository. Users should regenerate raw inputs from the original public providers using the scripts in `scripts/` and should follow each provider's license, terms of use, and attribution requirements.

## Generated Outputs

The included tables, figures, manifests, and manuscript files are generated research artifacts. They are included to document the submitted analysis and to make the computational trail inspectable.

## Provider Notes

- Open Asset Pricing files should be obtained from Open Asset Pricing or through the `openassetpricing` package where available; users should cite Chen and Zimmermann (2022), "Open Source Cross-Sectional Asset Pricing."
- FRED series should be obtained from FRED.
- Kenneth French Data Library series should be obtained from the Kenneth French Data Library.
- JKP Global Factor Data should be obtained from the JKP data provider, with attribution and license limits preserved.
